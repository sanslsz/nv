Overview

Importance

  1. The product detail pages represent 10% of the entire enterprise pages
  2. They have large impact on driving sales and search hits

Problems

  1. Poor UX: the pages’ inconsistencies make it more challenge to browse, increase the cognitive load for the users
  2. Effectiveness, performance and metrics can be improved
  3. Inefficiencies: large amount of resource goes into building the pages

Goals

  1. Better UX: consistent design, content, narrative/communication /storytelling, forming a predictable pattern so that it’s easy & quick to browse and identify information.
  2. Content & communication approach: clear, fast, easy read
  3. Drive more sales for non-OEM products: making the CTAs more prominent
  4. Efficiency: quick & easy to build with new Figma components & templates, pre-built specimen pages on the AEM platform
  5. Maintenance: capabilities, features, and design stay up to date with periodic reviews

Strategy

Problem Solution
Visual design Alignment Centered aligned text & icons. Align to left whenever possible
Text line width Full width is not the best reading experience, hard to track lines Cap body text’s max text line width at 75 characters to assist reading.
Spacing

Inconsistent spacing among items

Lack Separation / grouping

Use space tokens(xs-xxl)

New responsive grid in the future
Heading font-size

Large text size, appear crowded and bloated

<h2> is h-medium, <h3> is h-smaller

Smaller heading size

<h2>: h-small, <h3>: h-smallest
Color Green color for regular text not meeting WCAG 2 AA requirement Avoid using NV green for text
Graphic Images / icons are generic and don’t serve a purpose, creating visual noise and clutter

Icons / images that are generic can be omitted. Only use icons that serve functional purpose:

  1. Visually separate the content, assist reading / scanning
  2. Meaningful images that’s specific to the product or topic, enhancing the messaging & communication
Content length management Too much text & content Use accordion, slides, tabs to manage hide & show low priority and repetitive content
Copywriting Clutter Content is wordy, resulting in a bloated look. Audience may lose interest and focus while reading Word count or reading time est. (entire page takes max 5 mins to read)
Clarity Sections lack clear labels for its content, making it difficult to quickly scan the page Add a preheader to clarify the content within the section. Headings can be optional.
Content & structure Order Inconsistent content order Follow the PDP template’s section order for all product pages
Repeated content Purchase hero, intro sections may have content that’s repeated in other sections May repeat CTA links but not heading and copy text
Repeated sections Repeated use of the same component, layout and composition in adjacent & unrelated sections, creating unwanted tie and visual fatigue that may result in the users scroll past the section without knowing the content

Designers and authors try their best to avoid unwanted repetitions.

Have diverse layout and component options to choose from.
