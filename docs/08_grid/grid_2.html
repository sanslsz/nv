Designer quotes

  • Designed in response to the internal pressures of content and the outer edge or frame, an effective grid is not a rigid formula but a flexible and resilient structure, a skeleton that moves in concert with the muscular mass of information.

    Allen Lupton

  • Grid isn't a prison. It’s an underlying structure and a harmonious set of proportions. There’s a helpful set of guides that enable the designer to organize things with hierarchy, clarity and unity.

    Sean Adams

  • Simplicity, order and visual neutrality can facilitate inclusivity… but they also pose a potential threat through the aesthetic & intellectual conformity. The overused templates may lead to an experiential landscape that’s repetitive, undifferentiated and mind-numbingly dull.

    Timothy Samara
